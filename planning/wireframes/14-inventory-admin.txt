   - Form validation for receiving

   - Fallback to manual SKU entry

   - WebSocket or SSE for stock level updates
   - Alert notifications for low stock
   - Live count progress for team counts

5. REPORTING:
   - PDF generation for PO documents
   - Excel export for stock reports
   - Controlled substance audit reports

6. MOBILE OPTIMIZATION:
   - Touch-friendly large buttons

   - Offline capability for stock counts
   - Camera-first interface for scanning

7. PERMISSIONS:

   - Create PO: manager + owner

8. AUDIT TRAIL:
   - All movements logged with user, timestamp

   - Count discrepancies require explanation
   - PO approval workflow
