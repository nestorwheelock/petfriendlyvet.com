REQUIREMENTS DATABASE:
- Store requirements by destination country
- Update requirements periodically (regulations change)
- Include airline-specific requirements
- Support for EU Pet Passport format
- Support for USDA/APHIS format

CERTIFICATE GENERATION:
- PDF generation with clinic branding
- QR code for verification (links to digital copy)
- Digital signature capability
- Multiple language support (ES/EN)
- Barcode for microchip number

VALIDATION RULES:
- Rabies vaccine must be 30+ days old (most countries)
- Health exam within 10 days of travel (varies by country)
- Microchip must be ISO 11784/11785 compliant
- Age restrictions (some countries require 4+ months)

REMINDERS:
- Notify when approaching travel date
- Alert if certificate expires before return date
- Remind to schedule exam at correct time
- Send documents digitally after generation

INTEGRATIONS:
- Calendar export (Google, iCal, Outlook)
- Email delivery of documents
- WhatsApp delivery of documents
- Link to pet profile for document storage

COUNTRY-SPECIFIC REQUIREMENTS:
- USA: CDC requirements, APHIS 7001 form
- EU: EU Pet Passport, tapeworm treatment (UK)
- Canada: Rabies certificate
- Australia: Strict quarantine requirements (special handling)
- UK: Post-Brexit rules, different from EU
