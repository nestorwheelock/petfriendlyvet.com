NOTES:
- Cart uses HTMX for quantity updates (no page reload)
- Alpine.js for client-side cart state
- Stripe Elements for secure payment form
- Order confirmation sends email automatically
- WhatsApp integration for order questions
- Consider order tracking in future version
- IVA calculation: subtotal * 0.16
- Store pickup is free, delivery has fee
